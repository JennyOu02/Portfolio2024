Connect.edu - Matching System

#Project-management
#Unit-test
#UAT

Connect.edu is designed specifically for international educational institutions that offer multiple programmes, such as study abroad, summer trips, and J1 programmes. It provides accurate and customised matching based on specific requirements, ensuring that students are paired with the most suitable agents.

The system features a user-friendly interface that requires no IT background, allowing internal staff to operate it effortlessly. Administrators can set up the rules by adding matching categories and tags to each program. These categories and tags appear as questions and answers on forms that both students and agents complete. When a student books an appointment, they are automatically matched with the best agent option available for that program.

Plant CareTaker

#IoT
#Electrical-engineering
Arduino

What if you didn't have to worry about who would look after your plants when you are away on holiday? The Plant Caretaker is an automatic irrigation system that uses Arduino to water your indoor plants! This type of system can be useful for those who want to make sure their plants are healthy and receive the appropriate amount of water each day. Without over- or under-watering.

There are several features in the system. The plant caretaker checks the soil moisture level daily and if the soil is wet enough, a green LED will turn on indicating a watered plant. Whereas if the soil is dry, it will start watering the plant and make a sound, to show that it has successfully hydrated the plant. A red LED light appears if the water reserve has run out, alerting the user.
