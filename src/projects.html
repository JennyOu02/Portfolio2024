Background

Inspired by my experience at Jobwise, which operates under a global educational institution offering programmes such as study abroad, working holidays, and online English learning, I developed Connect.edu - an automated matching system. Besides its main services, the company also outsourced smaller services, like visa assistance and personal statement editing, to external firms. The services are aimed at students who often have very specific requirements, requiring agents who are well-matched to handle these needs. Traditionally, experienced sales managers handled the matching process informally. However, due to a shortage of IT staff, each service‚Äôs matching system was managed differently‚Äîeither on various platforms or manually‚Äîmaking the process inefficient, chaotic, and challenging to integrate into a unified system.

Target Users

  • Companies like global educational institutions that offer multiple programmes, where the accuracy of matching results directly impacts the company's reputation and client relationships.
  • Companies that collaborate with various sponsors or outsource to external firms, requiring careful pre-selection of clients to align with these external services.

  • Organisations with small IT departments, where services are not centralised within a single system, making integration challenging due to a lack of technical staff.
  • Small to mid-sized companies facing budget constraints that prevent them from implementing a CRM system for each service.
